Chess Learning

Learn about each chess piece, how they move, and listen to descriptions of their unique abilities!

King

The King moves one square in any direction. It's the most important piece; protect it at all costs!

Queen

The Queen can move any number of squares along a row, column, or diagonal. She is the most powerful piece.

Rook

The Rook moves in straight lines along rows and columns. It is very powerful in open lines.

Bishop

The Bishop moves diagonally, allowing it to cover only one color of squares throughout the game.

Knight

The Knight moves in an ‘L’ shape: two squares in one direction and then one square perpendicular.

Pawn

The Pawn moves forward one square, but captures diagonally. It can move two squares forward on its first move.
